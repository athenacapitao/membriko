### Section 3: Why EPDM for Curtain Wall Gaskets and Seals
- UV stability intrinsic to the EPDM polymer chain (saturated backbone)
- Ozone resistance: critical for Portuguese coastal and urban environments
- Compression set resistance: maintaining long-term sealing force without permanent deformation
- Temperature range (-45°C to +130°C): covering full Portuguese climate exposure on glazed facades
- Chemical inertness: compatibility with structural silicone, cleaning agents, aluminium anodising
