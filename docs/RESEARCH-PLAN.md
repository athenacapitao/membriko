# Membriko EPDM Research Plan - Ultimate Knowledge Base

## Overview
Research initiative to create the most comprehensive EPDM membrane knowledge base in Portuguese and English. Target: Become the #1 EPDM resource in Portugal.

## Research Tasks (20+ Topics)

### Phase 1: Core EPDM Science & Engineering
1. **EPDM Material Science** - Chemical composition, polymer structure, properties
2. **EPDM Manufacturing Process** - Production methods, quality control, standards
3. **EPDM vs Other Membranes** - Bituminous, PVC, TPO, EPDM comparisons
4. **EPDM Technical Specifications** - Thickness, durability, certifications (EN, ISO, ASTM)

### Phase 2: Applications Research
5. **Flat Roofs (Coberturas Planas)** - Installation techniques, details, case studies
6. **Pitched Roofs (Telhados Inclinados)** - Underlayment, valleys, penetrations
7. **Green Roofs (Coberturas Verdes)** - Root barriers, drainage, vegetation compatibility
8. **Terraces & Balconies (Terraços)** - Walkable surfaces, UV exposure
9. **Swimming Pools (Piscinas)** - Liner systems, chemical resistance, installation
10. **Lakes & Reservoirs (Lagos)** - Pond liners, water containment, durability
11. **Facades (Fachadas)** - Wall waterproofing, rain screen systems
12. **Foundations (Fundações)** - Underground waterproofing, damp proofing
13. **Industrial Buildings (Edifícios Industriais)** - Large-scale applications
14. **Commercial Buildings (Edifícios Comerciais)** - Retail, offices, logistics

### Phase 3: Regional & Market Research
15. **Portugal EPDM Market** - Suppliers, distributors, pricing
16. **Spain EPDM Market** - Cross-border suppliers, import considerations
17. **Climate Adaptations** - Portugal climate considerations (Atlantic, Mediterranean)

### Phase 4: Scientific & Engineering Papers
18. **Academic Research Papers** - Search Google Scholar, ScienceDirect, ResearchGate
19. **Engineering Standards** - EN 13956, EN 13967, related standards
20. **Case Studies & Technical Reports** - Real-world project data

### Phase 5: Technical Deep Dives
21. **Installation Best Practices** - Certified installer requirements, details
22. **Maintenance & Repair** - Lifecycle management, repair techniques
23. **Sustainability** - Recyclability, environmental impact, LCA studies
